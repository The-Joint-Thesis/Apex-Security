Core Features

Built to protect, designed to last.

Comprehensive protection through intelligent monitoring systems for the modern industrial workforce.

01

Electric Field Detection

Custom probes detect live conductors up to 5 m away, preventing electrical accidents before contact.

EF Sensor · GPIO ADC
02

Thermal Monitoring

MLX90614 IR sensors scan for hot surfaces and overheating equipment at 10 Hz continuously.

MLX90614 · I2C · 0x5A
03

Vital Signs Tracking

Heart rate and SpO₂ via MAX30102. Alerts fire if values leave safe thresholds.

MAX30102 · I2C · 0x57
04

Fall Detection

MPU6050 detects free-fall and impact, dispatching SOS after a 15-second confirmation window.

MPU6050 · I2C · 0x68
05

Multi-Modal Alerts

Haptic, buzzer, and LED channels fire across three priority levels simultaneously.

Haptic · Buzzer · LED
06

IoT Connectivity

BLE 5.0 for wrist-cuff telemetry and 802.11n WiFi for cloud dashboard sync via MQTT.

ESP32-S3 · BLE · WiFi

I2C Bus

SDA GPIO 21, SCL GPIO 22. Three I2C sensors share the bus. 4.7 kΩ pull-ups required on both lines.

Power Note

MAX30102 draws LED burst currents. A 10 µF bulk cap near its VCC pin is mandatory.
